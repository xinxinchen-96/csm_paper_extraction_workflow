The study uses weather data that includes daily minimum and maximum temperatures, solar radiation, precipitation, and potential evapotranspiration. The analysis was conducted using data from 1999 to 2019 for two locations in Northern France. The coordinates for these two main study sites are Beauvais (Lat. 49.465, Long. 2.072) and Estrées-Mons (Lat. 49.878, Long. -3.0067)【4:2†source】.

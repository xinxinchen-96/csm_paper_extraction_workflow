The study utilizes weather data from a meteorological station located about 750 meters from the Broadbalk site. This data includes daily rainfall measurements and air temperature records, along with other atmospheric inputs. Specifically, rainfall and temperature have been recorded in detail, with a mean annual rainfall of 704mm between 1870 and 2018 and annual air temperature records starting from 1878【4:1†source】.

The study is conducted at Rothamsted Research in Harpenden, Herts, United Kingdom. The geographical coordinates for Rothamsted are approximately 51.8079° N latitude and 0.3561° W longitude【4:10†source】.

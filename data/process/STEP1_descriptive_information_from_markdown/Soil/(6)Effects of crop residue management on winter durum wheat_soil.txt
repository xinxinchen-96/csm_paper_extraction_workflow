The study uses soil data from an experimental farm located in Foggia, Apulia region, southern Italy. The precise coordinates of the study location are 41°27' latitude N and 15°36' longitude E. The soil at this location is of clay-loam texture of alluvial origin, classified as fine, mesic, Typic Chromoxerert according to Soil Taxonomy-USDA【4:0†Effects of crop residue management on winter durum wheat】.

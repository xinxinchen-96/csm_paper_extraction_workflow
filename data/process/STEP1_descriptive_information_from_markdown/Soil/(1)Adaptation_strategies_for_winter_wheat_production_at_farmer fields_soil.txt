The study uses soil data collected at the Zhuozhou Experimental Station of China Agricultural University, which is located at 39°27′N latitude, 115°5′E longitude. The soil type is predominantly sandy, and the soil layer from 0 to 40 cm has a maximum accessible water content (AWC) of 0.16 cm³/cm³.
